about

  • MAKS (Maksym Chupov-Ryabtsev) is a 22 year old Ukrainian born producer and composer who is based in Toronto, Canada.

  • With his classical piano training, and a degree in Electroacoustic Composition from the University of Toronto, MAKS aims to extract the finest aspects of contrasting musical styles, cultures, and artistic practices in order to best inform his own musical processes and creative output.

  • MAKS is affiliated with the MSTRKRFT founded label "ORO" where he releases his music alongside his independent endeavours.
